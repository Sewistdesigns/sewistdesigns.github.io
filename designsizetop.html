Step 3

Fill out your measurements to create a made to measure pattern. Look at the diagram for a clearer visualisation, and read the description at the bottom to get accurate measurements. For specific instructions on how to change specifics of the pattern go to the ‘How to’ page.

It is important to stand straight while taking your measurements. It also makes it a lot easier if you have someone to help you take your measurements. When taking measurements, wear the underclothers you will wear under the the peace of clothing you are designing.
Around bust: around thickest part of the bust.
Around small bust: above the bust, under the arms.
Around thorax: around and under the bust.
Around waist: around thinnest part/waist.
First hip width: first thickest part of hip, often between 6 to 12 cm from waist.
Second hip width: thickest part of hip, often between 18 to 26 cm from waist.
Chest lenght: from the dip between your collarbones to waist.
Chest width: measured 5 cm below the dip between your collarbones, from armhole to armhole.
Bust height: from the dip between your collarbones to height of bust (imaginary line between nipples).
Bust seperation: from bustpoint to bustpoint (nipple to nipple).
Back lenght: from neck bobble to waist.
Back width: measured 8cm below the neck bobble, from armhole to armhole.
Shoulder width: from nack to end of shoulder.
Around collar: across neck bobble, shoulderpoints, and dip between collarbones.
Sleeve lenght to elbow: bend elbow, from schoulder to elbowtip.
Sleeve lenght to wrist: bend elbow, from schoulder, over elbowtip, to wrist.
Upper arm: around upperarm.
Elbow: around bend elbow.
Wrist: around wrist.
Hand: around hand.
