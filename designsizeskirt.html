Step 3

Fill out your measurements to create a made to measure pattern. Look at the diagram for a clearer visualisation, and read the description at the bottom to get accurate measurements. For specific instructions on how to change specifics of the pattern go to the ‘How to’ page.





It is important to stand straight while taking your measurements. It also makes it a lot easier if you have someone to help you take your measurements. When taking measurements, wear the underclothers you will wear under the the peace of clothing you are designing.
Around waist: around thinnest part/waist.
First hip width: first thickest part of hip, often between 6 to 12 cm from waist.
Second hip width: thickest part of hip, often between 18 to 26 cm from waist.


After sending your choices, press next to continue to the drawingpage.
